# metal

## Overview
Details about metal and its use

## Stainless steel (304)
Great for strength, the strongest of the others

0.30" (0.8mm)
- Strong, great for small reinforcement
- Application: Posts (good), shelves (when strength is needed)

0.48" (1.2mm)
- Extremely strong, great for the posts
- Application: Posts (great), shelves (when strength is needed)

## Galvanized (G90)
Has a protective zinc coating, is also fairly strong and easier to work with than stainless steel

0.30" (0.8mm)
- Strong and durable, easy to work with
- Applications: Chassis, Shelves, Front panel, Rack front/back/side panels, POCs

0.36" (0.9mm)
- Applications: Chassis, Shelves, Front panel

0.48" (1.2mm)
- Applications: Chassis, Shelves, Front panel

## Mild steel (A36/1008)
Extremely easy to work with, no protective coating, easy to bend by hand, rusts

Only useful for simple concepts
